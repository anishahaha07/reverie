About Us



Our Story

Welcome to Reverie, where culinary dreams come to life. Our story began with a simple idea: to create a dining experience that transcends the ordinary. Founded by a group of passionate food enthusiasts in 1980, Reverie quickly evolved from a humble concept to a beloved culinary destination.

Driven by a shared love for exceptional food and warm hospitality, our team set out to craft a menu that celebrates the diversity of flavors from around the world. Drawing inspiration from our travels, family recipes, and culinary trends, we curated a selection of dishes that showcase our dedication to quality, creativity, and innovation.

At Reverie, every dish tells a story – a story of passion, craftsmanship, and the joy of sharing delicious food with others. We invite you to embark on this culinary journey with us and discover the magic of Reverie for yourself.



Our Team

Welcome to the heart and soul of Reverie – our exceptional team. Comprised of passionate chefs, dedicated servers, and hospitality professionals, our team is the driving force behind the unforgettable dining experience we offer. Our chefs are culinary artisans, skilled in the art of transforming fresh, high-quality ingredients into culinary masterpieces that tantalize the taste buds and ignite the senses. With creativity and expertise, they craft each dish with precision and care, ensuring that every bite is a culinary delight.

But our team extends beyond the kitchen. Our servers are more than just waitstaff – they are ambassadors of hospitality, dedicated to providing personalized service and creating memorable moments for every guest. With warmth and attentiveness, they ensure that your dining experience at Reverie is nothing short of extraordinary.

Together, our team embodies the spirit of Reverie – a passion for excellence, a commitment to hospitality, and a dedication to creating unforgettable memories for our guests. We invite you to meet the faces behind the magic and experience the warmth and hospitality of our team firsthand at Reverie.

Experience the enchanting allure of Reverie, conveniently located on Silk Street in the vibrant heart of London City, just on the fringe of Soho. Our restaurant invites you to indulge in a culinary journey like no other, where every dish is crafted with passion and precision. Immerse yourself in our captivating ambiance, where urban energy meets timeless elegance, creating the perfect setting for a memorable dining experience. Join us at Reverie and savor the flavors of London in a truly unforgettable setting.

As you step into Reverie, you'll be welcomed by our warm and attentive staff, ready to guide you through a menu that celebrates the diversity of London's culinary scene. From innovative takes on classic British dishes to international flavors infused with local ingredients, there's something to tantalize every palate. Join us and let Reverie become your go-to destination for exquisite dining experiences in the heart of London.
